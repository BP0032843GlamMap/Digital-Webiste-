GLAM~MAP

Is

A Smart App…

GLAM~MAP is digital platform for make-up artists, hairstylists, nail technicians and eyebrow beauticians as a mean in marketing themselves through the app and especially as social medias has become an advance platform to market oneself and attracting consumers through the comfort of an app to locate and book preferred MUA or aesthetician of personal preference for any required occasion in advance or even in an emergency.

The app is considered to be an online resume of MUA's and aesthetician as it is easily accessible for potential consumers within this digitalise era of the 21st nearly every individual consists of having a smartphone, Ipad or tablet are able to access the service regardless of where the consumer is. They are able to book appointments on the go just by their current location instead of wasting valuable time in finding a hairstylists or makeup artists and book appointment in advance for various events such as weddings, birthdays, meetings, prom, or even a red-carpet event that individual desires to look impeccable for the night.

The portfolio on the artists' profile is their personal brand campaign in advertising themselves individually. Freelancer MUA's and aesthetician predominantly are required to have a portfolio consisting their previous work visible through videos and images for consumers to be able to review their previous work. This method will indicate to consumers, why MUA'S or aesthetician should be hired and booked, as it enables consumers to comprehend the degree of skill and capabilities the individual possess before booking them.
