fEATURES OF GLAM~MAP

Business Accounts

In comparison to Customers; Freelancers and MUA's will have a business account where, cosmetics productions and brands will also have business accounts on the app in relation to access in targeting makeup artists/ freelancers when having events and seminars in launching their new products. This will only be strictly advertised on the app for artists where they can sign-up and pay to go the event (this service will be only between make up production companies and artists or aestheticians, saloon owners, make up schools, salon owners - also it is a great means of networking between make-up artists and brand productions

With their business accounts Cosmetic and make-up production brands will have MUA's and freelancers to endorse their products, this will be through the means of MUAs and artists will use products on clients and be able to tag the products and items name used on their models, consumers can identify those tags and then directly from the app purchase these products and items. This process limits the dissatisfaction of consumers, instead of emailing the artists in regards to inquiring for the products and items used on the look, this improves the process and satisfies consumers inquiry.

Book Your Appointment

One of the features of the app is that it enables users to book an immediate appointment or schedule or for later. In relation to the availability of both consumers and the artists, depending on the date and time required by the individual; they will send a request through the app to the artists nearest available to them or to their personal preference. Artist's then shall be able to either accept or decline the request. Enabling alternative MUA artists to be shown available for convenience purposes in accepting and covering the booking.

An individual can book more than one Freelancer, depending on the nature of their requirement and event, to cover hair and make up for instant. Albeit, Artists can collaborate on the app with one another. For instance, individuals who are MUA's that do not cover nor specialise in hairdressing, or vice versa they can both collaborate as a duo together for consumers, or the consumer can select and book their desire artists individually for a specific date

Before confirmation, customer will have to select an option of whether the customer will meet the artist's location or require the artist(s) to meet at the current location of the customer. If the individual requires additional artists & freelancer then the required location will be of the customer as there will be more than one artists in attaining the demand of the customer. However, different charges will apply in relations to the location of the user, in regards to the requirement of Freelancers traveling tremendously outside their location circumference of

Once appointment is scheduled appointment is booked and provide method of payment, they can then provide their location to the artist. There will be a cancelation fee for all customer who have already booked and is cancelling within the 24 hours of the scheduled booking.

Ratings & Reviews

Seminars

MUAs and artists will be rated on their level of punctuality and professionalism. They will also be rates on the content of their portfolios and on the level of how efficiently and effectively present their work including, tags of products for customers, as these only influences artists to keep up to date with their portfolios with enhances and rich content ready for persuading consumers in booking and rating them for others. MUAs and Freelancer ratings and reviews will be shown publicly for the amount of booking they have taken and the number of products being purchased or products being redirected to cosmetic brands to be purchased.

They also be rated by customers on the level of their ability from each booked session, as how satisfied the individual was with the artists level of skill and ability.

Beauty seminars can be booked for beauty gurus or cosmetic brands holding seminars in selling tickets for seminar & workshops through the app. Make-up seminars will consist of beauty gurus and MUA's in having incentives for members in selling new products that yet still have not launched to the public world-wide.

The make-up seminars are a means of promotions platform for costemtic brands, individual MUA's and artists to promote the new launch of thier product line, and provide individuals attending with the awareness of the products availability or provide testers of the launched products.
